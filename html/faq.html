Registering and Logging in

To register for an account, click the "login / register" button in the top-left corner, type in your email address and create a password (must include at least 1 number and 1 symbol) then click "register", once your account is registered press the "login" button and proceed with you action

Forgot your password?

To reset your password, please use the contact function by clicking here, please ensure you list your registered email address, within 24 hours, you should recieve a password reset link via your email

Tracking todays progress

Once logged in, click the "tracker" button on the navigation bar and fill in todays date and other fields. Once done, click "save progress" and wait for it to be confirmed

Reflecting on previous progress

To look over and reflect on the progress, click "progress" in the navigation bar, from here, you can see a table with each date and fieldset listed

Logging out

To logout, simply click the "logout" button on the top-right
